Spaghetti with Meatballs

Description

A classic Italian-American comfort dish featuring tender, juicy meatballs simmered in a rich tomato sauce, served over a bed of al dente spaghetti. The hearty meatballs, often made with a blend of ground meats, breadcrumbs, and flavorful herbs, perfectly complement the savory, tangy sauce. This satisfying meal is a family favorite, ideal for weeknight dinners or special gatherings, and is best enjoyed with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and fresh basil.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup breadcrumbs
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meatball Mixture
  2. Form the Meatballs
  3. Cook the Meatballs
  4. Make the Sauce
  5. Simmer the Sauce
  6. Add Meatballs to the Sauce
  7. Cook the Spaghetti
  8. Combine Spaghetti and Sauce
  9. Serve
